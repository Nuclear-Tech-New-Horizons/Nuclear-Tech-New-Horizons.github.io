About NTNH

Nuclear Tech: New Horizons (NTNH) is an industrial hi-tech modpack for Minecraft 1.7.10, inspired by the legendary GTNH. Dive into a world of complex machinery, nuclear power, and hardcore progression. Perfect for players who love a challenge!
